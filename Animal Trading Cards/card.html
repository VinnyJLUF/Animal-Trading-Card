African Lion

Interesting fact about African Lion is that in Swahili, an African Language, is 'simba'.

  • Groups: Lions are the only cats that live in groups.
  • Number of lions in group: A group, or pride, can be up to 30 lions, depending on how much food and water is available.
  • Roles: Female lions are the main hunters. While they're out looking for food, the males guard the pride's territory and their young.
  • Lion's roar: It can be heard up to eight kilometres away.
  • Lion's territory: Lions scent mark their territory, using their wee, to create a border.
